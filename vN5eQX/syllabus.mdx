# Deep Learning Fundamentals

## Overview
In this first week, we will lay the foundation for your experience by providing a basic understanding of core concepts in deep learning, BCI, and brain imaging analysis. We will explore the physics and math behind EEG/MEG and signal processing, as well as familiarize ourselves with popular libraries for EEG/MEG/fMRI analysis. By the end of this week, you will have a solid understanding of the fundamental concepts and tools necessary for the rest of the experience.

## The Group Experience
During this week, you will participate in a hands-on workshop where you will have the opportunity to apply the concepts learned in a practical setting. You will also engage in stimulating discussions with your group members to deepen your understanding of the topics covered.

## Outcomes
- Basic understanding of core concepts in deep learning, BCI, and brain imaging analysis
- Familiarity with the physics and math behind EEG/MEG and signal processing
- Knowledge of popular libraries for EEG/MEG/fMRI analysis (e.g., braindecode and MNE)

# Machine Learning Pipelines for EEG/MEG Data Processing

## Overview
Building upon the knowledge gained in the previous week, we will now delve into machine learning pipelines for EEG/MEG data processing. We will explore different techniques and algorithms used in processing and analyzing brain imaging data. By the end of this week, you will be able to design and implement machine learning pipelines specifically tailored for EEG/MEG data.

## The Group Experience
During this week, you will participate in an intense hackathon where you will work collaboratively with your group members to develop machine learning pipelines for EEG/MEG data processing. This hands-on experience will allow you to apply your knowledge and skills in a real-world scenario.

## Outcomes
- Ability to design and implement machine learning pipelines for EEG/MEG data processing

# Application of Machine Learning in Neuroscience and Healthcare

## Overview
In this week, we will explore the application of machine learning in neuroscience and healthcare. We will examine how machine learning techniques can be used to analyze brain imaging data and make predictions related to neurological disorders and healthcare outcomes. By the end of this week, you will have a comprehensive understanding of the potential applications of machine learning in these domains.

## The Group Experience
During this week, you will engage in stimulating discussions with your group members to explore the various applications of machine learning in neuroscience and healthcare. You will also have the opportunity to work on a collaborative project that applies machine learning techniques to real-world datasets.

## Outcomes
- Understanding of the application of machine learning in neuroscience and healthcare

# Research Experience in Data Science and Deep Learning

## Overview
Building upon the knowledge and skills acquired in the previous weeks, we will now focus on research experience in data science and deep learning. We will explore different research methodologies used in clinical research and substance use disorders. By the end of this week, you will have a solid understanding of research methodologies and be prepared to apply them in your own research projects.

## The Group Experience
During this week, you will participate in a one-on-one challenge-solving session with a fellow group member. This session will provide an opportunity for you to apply your research skills and collaborate with your partner to solve a research problem related to data science and deep learning.

## Outcomes
- Knowledge of research methodologies in clinical research and substance use disorders

# Working with Open-Source Datasets and Experimenting with Projects

## Overview
In this final week, we will focus on working with open-source datasets from platforms like OpenNeuro. We will explore different datasets and experiment with rudimentary projects in EEG/MEG data processing and machine learning pipelines. By the end of this week, you will have hands-on experience working with real-world datasets and be able to independently experiment with projects in the field.

## The Group Experience
During this week, you will have the opportunity to showcase your projects and receive feedback from your group members. This collaborative environment will allow you to learn from each other's experiences and further enhance your skills.

## Outcomes
- Ability to work with open-source datasets from platforms like OpenNeuro
- Experience in experimenting with rudimentary projects in EEG/MEG data processing and machine learning pipelines.
