## The Roadmap
The experience is structured in a logical sequence, with each week building upon the previous one to advance your knowledge and skills. Here's an outline of the roadmap and flow of the experience:

1. **Week 1: Improved Coding Skills**
   - Focus on enhancing your coding skills through practice and learning resources.
   - Core concepts understanding to lay a strong foundation for the upcoming topics.
   - Engage in basic coding projects or tasks to apply your skills.

2. **Week 2: Knowledge and Understanding of Transformer Models**
   - Dive into the core concepts of Transformer models, including attention mechanisms and self-attention.
   - Explore different types of Transformer architectures and their applications.
   - Engage in basic projects or tasks related to Transformer models to solidify your understanding.

3. **Week 3: Knowledge and Understanding of Natural Language Processing (NLP)**
   - Gain knowledge and understanding of NLP techniques, such as tokenization, word embeddings, and language modeling.
   - Explore common NLP tasks like sentiment analysis, named entity recognition, and text classification.
   - Engage in basic NLP projects or tasks to apply your knowledge.

4. **Week 4: Knowledge and Understanding of Reinforcement Learning**
   - Delve into the core concepts of reinforcement learning, including Markov decision processes, value functions, and policy optimization.
   - Explore different algorithms and techniques used in reinforcement learning, such as Q-learning and policy gradients.
   - Engage in basic projects or tasks related to reinforcement learning to reinforce your understanding.

5. **Week 5: Familiarity with Research Methodologies and Techniques**
   - Learn about research methodologies and techniques commonly used in the field.
   - Understand how to formulate research questions, design experiments, and analyze results.
   - Gain familiarity with reading and understanding research papers.

6. **Week 6: Applying Coding and Research Skills in Practical Projects**
   - Apply your coding and research skills in practical projects related to Transformer models, NLP, and reinforcement learning.
   - Collaborate with your group members to work on challenging projects and solve real-world problems.
   - Enhance your ability to implement complex projects and tasks.
